Our Commitment to Your Success

01
What types of immigration cases do you handle?

We handle a wide range of immigration cases, including family sponsorship, economic immigration, refugee claims, work permits, study permits, and citizenship applications.

02
How much do your services cost?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of the case. We offer initial consultations to discuss your specific needs and provide a fee estimate.

03
How long will my immigration process take?

Processing times vary depending on the type of application and the current processing times of Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC). We will provide you with an estimated timeline during your consultation.

04
What documents do I need for my application?

The required documents vary depending on the type of application. We will provide you with a detailed checklist of the necessary documents.

04
Do you handle human rights cases?

Yes, we provide legal assistance to those whose human rights have been violated.
